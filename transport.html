Ambulance Medical Service provides 24/7 transport within Singapore for emergency and non-emergency cases. We are registered under SCDF’s 1777 non-emergency ambulance hotline.

A non-emergency ambulance team consists of 1 Ambulance, an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) certified driver and a paramedic level 1 or 2 with BCLS +/- ACLS and ITLS certification.

An emergency ambulance team will have a paramedic level 3 (instead of level 1 or 2) present. This paramedic will also have BCLS +/- ACLS and ITLS certification.

We can transport critically ill patients requiring life support such as ventilators and infusion pumps.

Local transport

We provide one-way or two-way emergency and non-emergency transport to our patients’ destination. Special rates are available for regular transportation e.g. dialysis sessions, rehabilitation therapy.

Transport of Critically Ill

We can provide transport of critically ill patients with doctor/nurse accompaniment as requested. Our ICU equipment include:

- Dräger Oxylog® 3000 – for ventilation
- B.Braun Space Infusor – for accurate, controlled drug and fluids administration
- Philips Intellivue X2 monitor – for continuous vital sign monitoring and recording

The above are in addition to the comprehensive equipment aboard our ambulances. The full equipment list can be seen here.

Please contact our operation managers for details.
