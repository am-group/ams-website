Welcome to Ambulance Medical Service!

We have been serving Singaporeans since 1999, and we’re dedicated to serving our customers with premier pre-hospital care and a smile. Come aboard with our friendly and experienced crew and let us look after you!

These are the services that AMS offers:

Transport
24 hours Emergency or Non-Emergency
One-Way or Two-Way Regular Transport for dialysis sessions / therapy
Transport for Psychiatric patients
Airport Tarmac, Ferry Terminal
Overseas transport to West Malaysia, Batam, Bintan
Transport of Critically Ill
Cash and NETS payment accepted!
Event Standby
Engage an Ambulance with Paramedics, Nurses, Doctors
Medical coverage of Sports Events, Gatherings, Conventions, Exhibitions
First Aid station and Mobile Medical team on buggies, bicycles and motorcycles
Sales & Rental
Rent Oxygen cylinders of various sizes for home therapy
Purchase HeartSine Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs)
